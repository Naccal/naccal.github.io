Welcome, sit a while and listen.
Zorai Naccal

My name is Zorai Naccal, I'm not quite a designer but I am a dreamer, I make things that I see in my dreams, and though they may not be great, they are in fact, part of me.


To know more about me... we have to go a little further back!

I started doodling when I was very young, but it wasn't always my passion to create little doodles and to be honest its still more of a hobbie than a job!, when I went to University I studied Game Design Production Management, and ended up with some pretty fancy letters at the end of my name, but.. You'll never see a game made by me, Why?, because I didn't feel as though it were my calling.

No, I always felt my calling lay elsewhere but I never quite managed to figure it out until that little bit later, I've always helped people, that's the reason I wanted to become a Game Designer, I could spread joy with my games, allow people to follow stories that I followed when I was younger, stories that made me feel... But, life has its way of chancing things, as I'm sure you know. So I went back to education... And now I'm learning to be a mental health nurse, While still doodling on the side.... After-all, who wants to give up doodles!!


So what is this website all about I hear you roar, well I simply want to show off some of the art i've created over the years, and I hope you enjoy it, I take commissions and make a wide variety of things, Maps, Tiles, assets for things, strange little icons and I'm getting good at drawing in a new lineless style I've been developing, so its all a bit of fun really!


Please, sit back and enjoy my work as it appears on this website, slowly over time.
